Instructions

To use the classifier to predict protein subcellular locations from single IHC image:

  • paste your IHC image into the text area, or
  • upload a IHC image with your protein.

IHC image must be the same format like sample

Press the "Submit" button to upload the IHC image and begin the classification.

Waiting a moment and your results will be presented in web page.
