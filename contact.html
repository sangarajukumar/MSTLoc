Overview

    Founded in 2001, the School of Software, Shandong University is one of the first batch of National Demonstrative Software Institutes in China. The School is located at the Software Campus of Shandong University, adjacent to Jinan Software Park in the High-Tech Development District.

    There are 119 employees at the School, including 71 faculty members, of which 28 are professors and 25 are PhD advisors. Currently over 1872 students are enrolled in various programs offered by the School, including 71 doctoral students, 312 master students and 1489 undergraduate students.

    The School offers an accredited PhD program and a postdoctoral training station in Software Engineering, as well as an interdisciplinary PhD program in Artificial Intelligence. The School has built five major disciplines, which are human computer interactions and virtual reality, software and data engineering, visual media and intelligent processing, information security, and artificial intelligence.

    The School participates extensively in international collaborations, and has established close academic exchange and relationships with over 30 universities, research institutes, and multinational companies. In July 2019, the Joint SDU-NTU Centre for Artificial Intelligence Research(C-FAIR) was established, turning itself into a hub of high-end talents and scientific research in the field of artificial intelligence.
